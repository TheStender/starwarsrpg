Session Recaps

Session One - March 21, 2024

The players introduced their characters. They were on the Sith Cruiser Inferno, a YG-4210 Light Freighter above the planet of Korriban. Participating in the battle against the Republic their ship took heavy fire and all of the crew were killed except for the four players.

When Jack's character tried to pilot the ship the engines were not working. Thom discovered a fire in the engine room and vented the atmosphere to put the fire out, which worked.

A small Republic ship docked with the Inferno and two Republic soldiers boarded but were prompty killed.

The players drug all the dead bodies onto the Republic ship, got the memory core from it, and detached it.

Mike's character got a transmission from Lord Vitiate ordering them to a set of coordinates. They followed the order and entered hyperspace.

Session Two - March 28, 2024

You met with Lord Vishiate who is now in control of the Sith Empire, and is planning a strategic retreat to the unknown regions. In order to slip away undetected, he has tasked you with destroying a Republic sensor array on a nearby planet. You were not his first choice as he wanted the Captain of the Inferno, who was killed in the battle of Korriban. Since he had no one else he was forced to send you. He sent Lord Teris, his fourth in command, with you to assist.

You were told of the back door to the array, which was far less guarded than the front. Upon recon you found only a handful of guards, which you dispatched with ease.

Lord Teris planted explosives, and the sensor array was destroyed. You returned to the fleet with your mission being a success.

Session Three - April 4, 2024

Upon return to the Sith fleet you were instructed to retreive a holocron from a ship above Korriban. The ship was rendered inoperable and is floating derelict in space.

You exited hyperspace far enough from the Republic fleet that was still at the planet, and through Chester's piloting ability were able to hide behind the ship as it rotated. Chester docked, and you began searching the ship.

The engine room was searched first, where a crew member was found hiding and whimpering for his life, but Elysian put an end to the noise by slicing his throat.

Your search led you forward on the shift to the cargo bay, where two pirates were searching for something. You attacked them and easily killed them. The search for the holocron will continue next time...

Session Four - April 11, 2024

You fought your way to the cockpit of the ship. After a quick battle with the remaining pirates, Derriphan found the holocron but hid it. Leen went with Lord Teris to the cafeteria and found the last remaining crew member, who they promptly killed.

Leen and Lord Teris went to return to the cockpit when Leen shot Teris from behind. Other members of the party assisted Leen in killing Teris. The players left the ship with the holocron and Derriphan was able to access it to begin unlocking its secrets.

You were awarded 20 XP

Session Five - April 25, 2024

You all felt like there was no plan and life itself had no meaning. Perhaps as if you were being forced into some sort of a transition session. Chaos erupted and the universe's creator laughed with glee as you turned on each other.

Both Derriphan and Elysian mediated with the holocron. Derriphan informed the rest of the players that Lord Kressh himself had tasked them with opposing Lord Vitiate as he feared the new Sith leader's plan was to destroy all of the Dark Lords using a powerful ritual to drain their force essence and use it for himself.

Kressh informed Derriphan of a small set of followers, weak in the force, that he could use as he pleased. Also he thinks the third in command of the Empire, Lord Villen, might be willing to oppose Vitiate if he knew of the plan to drain his force power.

You made your way to Etti IV, the capital of the Corporate Sector, where Derriphan sold a lightsaber and purchased a droid. The droid attempted to pilot the ship off planet, but Chester was able to shut him down remotely and take control over the ship. After much arguing you set off to meet with the Sith fleet, to meet with Lord Villen and compose your course of action from there. The transition session ended not with a bang, but with a whimper. At least Thom got some action during the game.

Session Six - May 2, 2024

You met up with the Sith fleet, and made your way to Lord Vitiate. You were straightforward and honest with him, telling him the truth about the holocron and your knowledge of his force drain ritual. He said you were beneath his notice to be worried about having your force powers (for those that have them) being drained. He asked you for help in locating a suitable planet for his ritual. He sent you to Lord Villen.

You told Lord Villen about Vitiate's plan and Villen agreed he must be stopped. He said he would find the planet, but said you should go to Nathema where Lord Vitiate has been studying for the past several years. His residence there may have clues to the ritual. With both knowledge of the ritual and the right planet to work against Vitiate, Villen thinks you have a chance to stop him.

You will make your way to Nathema, gain entrance to Vitiate's residence, and locate the ritual information.

You were awarded 20 XP

Session Six Point Five - May 9, 2024

After the other players dropped from the call suddenly and without much warning, Chester, the fearless Chadra-Fan pilot, took center stage in an adventure that would test not only his piloting skills but also his wit and resourcefulness. With a daring grin etched across his furry visage, Chester bid his fellow companions aboard the Star Hawk farewell, itching for a solo escapade through the cosmos.

Setting his course for the bustling metropolis of Nar Shaddaa, the notorious smuggler's moon, Chester's nimble ship sliced through the star-strewn expanse, its engines humming with anticipation. Upon arrival, the neon-lit streets greeted him with a chaotic symphony of alien tongues and the tantalizing aroma of exotic spices.

Eager to immerse himself in the vibrant underworld, Chester navigated the labyrinthine alleys with finesse, his diminutive stature allowing him to slip past the notice of more cumbersome beings. His sharp senses and quick reflexes proved invaluable as he danced through the shadows, evading the watchful eyes of local enforcers and cunning bounty hunters alike.

Embracing the thrill of the unknown, Chester found himself entangled in a high-stakes sabacc game within the confines of a smoke-filled cantina. With a twinkle in his eye and a sly grin, he charmed his way into the game, his adept piloting skills translating seamlessly into the art of strategic gambling. Amidst the tension and excitement, Chester emerged victorious, his pockets lined with credits and his reputation as a daring gambler solidified.

But Chester's adventure was far from over. Fuelled by adrenaline and a thirst for adventure, he accepted a daring smuggling job that promised riches beyond his wildest dreams. Maneuvering his ship through treacherous asteroid fields and evading the relentless pursuit of Republic patrols, Chester proved himself to be a master of the stars, his daring escapades immortalized in the annals of galactic folklore.

As the twin suns dipped below the horizon, casting a golden hue across the endless expanse, Chester returned to his comrades aboard the Star Hawk, his heart ablaze with the thrill of his solo escapade. Though his journey had been perilous and fraught with danger, Chester emerged triumphant, a testament to the indomitable spirit of the galaxy's bravest adventurers.

Session Seven - May 16, 2024

You arrived at the private residence of Lord Vitiate, located on the planet Nathema. Setting your ship down under the guise of an inspection you were able to meet with Sir Vant, the manor's main servant. He gave you a quick tour of the mansion, but said the study was off limits to anyone but Lord Vitiate.

You had Vant start on a dinner that would include yourselves, the Duke in charge of the manor, and a Sith Inquisitor who was staying at the mansion. While he began preparing the meal, Chester and Elysian broke into the study, setting off an alarm. Leen, who had climbed to the roof, saw two troopers and then a speeder rapidly approaching the manor. Leen shot at the speeder driver, forcing him to crash. The dark-robed figure began walking slowly towards the manor.

Two troopers entered the gallery and began combat with Chester and Elysian. Leen dropped down from the roof behind the Inquisitor, while Derriphan entered the gallery. The group made quick work of the three, killing the two troopers and rendering the Inquisitor unconcious. After some sidetracking of a dinner, the group decided to grab a scroll that Elysian had seen in the study before closing it. Derriphan confirmed it looked like the ritual information and the group left the planet.

Arriving at Drommund Kaas, Lord Villen met with the group. After a quick study of the scroll he thought he would be able to counter Vitiate's ritual. You headed to the surface where in a large ampitheater area thousands of Sith had already gathered to hear a speech from Vitiate, who stood on a central platform. Leen climbed to the top of a nearby cliff.

The group appraoched Vitiate, and Leen fired on Villen. Villen took that opportunity to get close to Vitiate and began countering the ritual. Vitiate completed the ritual and a force bubble appeared around the platform, and all the Sith audience members seemed to go comatose. Villen collapsed, and Vitiate attacked the group. Weakened by Villen's counter he was not able to bring his full power against the group, but was able to defeat them with a small help from Leen who was able to penetrate the force bubble and fire on Chester. One by one the group fell from Vitiate's attacks.

As one of his final actions, Elysian armed a thermal detonator. As Leen climbed back onto the cliff, he saw an explosion consume the bubble. It collapased in on itself, and when it dissipated there was no trace of anything on the platform. No damage from the explosion or body parts. Leen watched as the thousands of Sith collapsed as they all died together.

What happened inside the bubble? Were the occupants vaporized by the thermal detonation? Why was the ritual seemingly completed as the force users all died as one? Leen can think about those questions as he grabs a ship and heads offworld.

To Be Continued...?

The Sith Empire is now without a leader, and thousands of their members lay dead on the surface of Drommund Kaas. Only one survivor remains to tell the tale, but will he speak of it, or forever keep the knowledge of what happened to himself?
